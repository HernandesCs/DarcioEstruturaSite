I am currently an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Oklahoma and Research Affiliate at the Institute for the Study of Labor (IZA). I graduated with a PhD in Economics from Duke University in May 2015.

My research interests include labor economics, urban economics, the economics of education, and applications of machine learning.
